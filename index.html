Sushant Singh Rajput

Sushant Singh Rajput (21 January 1986 - 14 June 2020) was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema.

List of movies done by Sushant Singh Rajput:

  • 2013 - Kai Po Che!, Shuddh Desi Romance
  • 2014 - PK
  • 2015 - Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!
  • 2016 - M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story
  • 2017 - Raabta
  • 2018 - Welcome to New York, Kedarnath
  • 2019 - Sonchiriya, Chhichhore, Drive
  • 2020 - Dil Bechara
